Working as a Financial Engineer at a hedge fund gave me practical work experience after college. I worked alongside the risk management team to develop new cashflow models for the Alternative Credit & Fixed Income (ACFI) business. I learned foundational concepts such as strong correlations in tails and the importance of diversification. My favorite project involved leveraging my programming skills to deploy a python package for the front office. In doing so, I learned multi-name credit modeling from senior researchers and container orchestration from computer engineers.
